Exercise 2: Get All Non-Buying Customers

Exercise:

Select the last name and first name of customers and the name of their favorite color for customers with no purchases.

Query:

SELECT 
    cu.last_name,
    cu.first_name,
    col.name AS favorite_color
FROM customer cu
LEFT JOIN color col 
    ON cu.favorite_color_id = col.id
LEFT JOIN clothing_order co 
    ON cu.id = co.customer_id
WHERE co.id IS NULL;
